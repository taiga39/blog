I bought a used keyboard on Mercari.

The seller said it was in beautiful condition, but the keycaps were actually pretty dirty. I kind of regret buying it.

So I started looking for replacement keycaps. But they aren't that cheap, so at that point it almost feels better to just buy a new keyboard.

Still, searching for keycaps has been interesting. There are so many colors and designs, and I also learned that keycaps come in different profiles and materials.
