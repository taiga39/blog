title: I removed the smart device from my wrist.
date: 2026-03-28
excerpt: After six years of using a Fitbit, I decided to stop wearing it. Most features weren't necessary, and I want to reduce charging cables as part of my desk-cleaning project.
---

I removed the smart device from my wrist.

I have used a Fitbit for six years.

It was useful for notifications, alarms, timers, and counting steps.
But almost all of those features are not really necessary.

Sleep logs are similar to how I feel, so it is not necessary to measure them.

However, a digital timer on my wrist was useful for cooking and keeping track of waiting time at cafés.
But because I could change the alarm time quickly, I often went back to sleep.

I've also been working on a desk-cleaning project, so I want to reduce the number of charging cables.
