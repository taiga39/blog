I have been studying English since the New Year.

However, I caught a cold about a week ago, so I had to stop.
I felt discouraged.

When I stop studying for some reason, I usually find it difficult to restart.

But three days ago, when I started to feel better, I began studying English again.
This is an important step forward for me.

Even though my study time is short, I believe that continuing is important.
