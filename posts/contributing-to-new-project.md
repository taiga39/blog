Even if you joined the project recently, you can still contribute.
When people join a new project, they often worry about how to contribute.
But there is no perfect team, so your knowledge can always be used.
For example, if you belonged to a big team before you changed jobs, you have knowledge about building large teams.
Engineers don't just write code, so you should share your knowledge.
